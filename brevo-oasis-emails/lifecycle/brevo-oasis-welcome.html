Here is your first mission in the Oasis

Three steps that make the biggest difference in your first session:

1. Import from your other browser

Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Brave — migrate history, bookmarks, tabs, and passwords into Oasis instantly. We never see this data; it stays on your device.
✦

2. Run your first AI command

Open the assistant and try one prompt — research, summarize a page, or ask anything.

3. Submit a training

Train the assistant on your workflow. You earn +1,000 extra tokens every time you train.
