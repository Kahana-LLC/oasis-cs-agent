Here is your first mission in the Oasis — three steps for your first session:

1. Import from your other browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Brave). Migrate history, bookmarks, tabs, and passwords into Oasis instantly. We never see this data; it stays on your device.

2. Run your first AI command. Open the assistant and try one prompt.

3. Submit a training. Train the assistant on your workflow. You earn +1,000 extra tokens every time you train.
