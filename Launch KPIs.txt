Here's a comprehensive list of metrics to monitor during and after your Product Hunt launch for Oasis:
Activation & Engagement Metrics
* AI command activation rate: % of new users who submit their first AI prompt within 1 hour, 24 hours, 3 days, and 7 days of signup
* Time to first AI command: Median and average time from signup to first prompt submission
* AI usage frequency: Average number of prompts per user per day (segmented by cohort)
* Power user identification: % of users submitting 10+ prompts in their first day/week
* Feature adoption depth: % of users who use AI commands in multiple sessions (not just once)
Retention & Churn Metrics
* D1, D3, D7, D14, D30 retention rates: % of users who return on each day post-signup
* Weekly active users (WAU): Tracking week-over-week growth
* Churn rate: % of users who don't return within 7/14/30 days
* Session frequency: Average sessions per active user per week

   * Feedback quality: Manual review sampling of submissions
