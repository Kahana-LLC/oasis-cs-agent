Oasis corporate goals (dashboard targets)
======================================

These goals drive the "Corporate goals" strip and goal-aware metric tooltips on the live dashboard.

Targets
-------
- Paid subscribers: 461 (Plus / active paid plans; metric: premium_users)
- Gross margin: 80%
  - Formula: (revenue − estimated API cost − Supabase $25/mo) / revenue
- DAU growth: 4.5× vs Product Hunt launch-week average
  - Launch date: May 27, 2026, 3:00 AM Eastern
  - Baseline: average daily DAU over the first 7 calendar days post-launch (auto-locked in Supabase)

Launch context
--------------
- Waitlist: 176 (manual constant until integrated from DB)
- Product Hunt: expect roughly 200–2,000 new signups at launch

Operations
----------
- Run daily: .venv/bin/python main.py --baseline
  - Populates baseline_metric_history for deltas
  - May lock DAU baseline after launch week
- Hover ? on any metric for: what it means, trend vs last week, how it relates to these goals
